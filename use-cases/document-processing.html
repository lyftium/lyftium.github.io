How Document Processing Learning Loop Systems Work

1. Upload Documents

Ingest PDFs, images, scanned documents, and digital files. The system processes documents from email attachments, cloud storage, or direct uploads.

2. AI Learns Your Formats

The learning loop system analyzes your document templates, field locations, and business rules. It adapts to different supplier formats and document variations.

3. Intelligent Data Extraction

Extract structured data from unstructured documents using vision AI and pattern recognition. Identify key fields, tables, and relationships automatically.

4. Validation & Enrichment

Validate extracted data against business rules, cross-reference with databases, and enrich with additional information. Flag anomalies for human review.

5. Output Integration

Export processed data to your ERP, accounting system, or database. Generate reports, trigger workflows, or update records automatically.
