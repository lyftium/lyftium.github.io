Yes. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime with no long-term commitment. Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation for smooth transition.

How long does implementation take?

Single projects typically take 2 weeks. Ongoing automation starts showing results within 4-6 weeks. We focus on delivering quick wins while building long-term value.

Yes. Annual commitments receive a 15% discount. Contact us for custom enterprise pricing and multi-year agreements.
